About Onam

Celebrated in Kerala

  • Onam, the vibrant festival of Kerala, bathes the land in a riot of colors and festivities.
  • It's a time of joy, with lively decorations, delicious feasts, and the iconic Pookalam flower arrangements.
  • The grand Snake Boat Race adds excitement to the celebrations, attracting crowds from far and wide.
  • Onam is a cultural extravaganza, where traditions come alive, and happiness knows no bounds.
  • People also commemorate the legendary King Mahabali's return, symbolizing love, benevolence, and equality.
